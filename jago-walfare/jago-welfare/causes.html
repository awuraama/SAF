Our Mission

The mission of SarkAkyere Foundation (SAF) is to give hope and build a better future for others by investing in child well-being, churches, orphanages, prisons and providing help in diverse ways.

SAF believes that humanity deserves the best and children especially deserve a future. Such concept of SAF is the core principles for humanity supports. We are transforming the way support for humanity is delivered, putting trained professionals to work for humanity in crisis anywhere of this world. SAF is focused on championing gender equality and transforming lives.

The SarkAkyere Foundation (SAF) operates the following grant-making programs:

Child Well – Being: SAF believes that children have the right to live right, transformed and be educated. SAF focuses on empowering children and ensuring they live to establish their God given talents.

Churches: SAF has over the years partnered with churches to help transform lives. This partnership seeks to improve love for humanity and to foster an inclusive conservative movement.

Orphanages: SAF continuously seeks to support orphanages to ensure children have a happy life in challenging moments of their lives. SAF focuses on helping more children in crises to recover and returning to positive transformative ways.

Prisons: SAF is working with prisons to help positively train inmates to become reformed for our societies. SAF believes that inmates have the right to education, well – being and reformed towards positive ways.

Community: SAF is working with all professionals to increase mutual understanding of human life and well – being among diverse populations to foster stronger and inclusive communities in Africa and beyond.
