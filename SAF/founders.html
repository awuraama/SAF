Founders

This foundation was seeded by Mr Richard Sarkodie Amo, IEng in 2020 post the COVID-19 season. He started this foundation to support the needy and giving them hope for the future. Mrs Ama Akyere Amo (his Wife) joined him after marriage to support the foundation and to advance it for humanity. The blessed couple are following their God given mission of helping others in diverse ways and are committed to doing so wholeheartedly.
